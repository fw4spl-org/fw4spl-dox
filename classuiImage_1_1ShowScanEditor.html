void uiImage::ShowScanEditor::configuring ( ) throw (fwTools::Failed) [protected, virtual]

Configure the editor.

Example of configuration 

     <service uid="showScanNegato3DEditor" type="::gui::editor::IEditor" impl="::uiImage::ShowScanEditor" autoConnect="no">
         <negatoAdaptor uid="myNegatoMPR" />
     </service>

myNegatoMPR is the uid of the visuVTKAdaptor::NegatoMPR service where the scan will be show/hide.
