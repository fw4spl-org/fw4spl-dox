Detailed Description

This class allows to register all the service configuration which has the point extension "::fwServices::registry::ServiceConfig".

Registry config like :

1 <extension implements="::fwServices::registry::ServiceConfig">
2  <id>SDBOpenIOSelectorConfig</id>
3  <service>::uiIO::editor::SIOSelector</service>
4  <desc>"Open" action's IOSelector config</desc>
5  <config>
6  <type mode="reader" />
7  <selection mode="include" />
8  <addSelection service="::ioAtoms::SReader" />
9  <config id="MDAtomsConfig" service="::ioAtoms::SReader" />
10  </config>
11 </extension>
