void uiImage::SliceListEditor2::configuring ( ) throw (fwTools::Failed) [protected, virtual]

Configure the editor.

Example of configuration 

     <service uid="sliceListNegato3DEditor" type="::gui::editor::IEditor" impl="::uiImage::SliceListEditor2" autoConnect="yes">
         <negatoAdaptor uid="myNegatoMPR" slices="1"/>
     </service>
  • uid is the uid of the visuVTKAdaptor::NegatoMPR service where the scan will be show/hide.
  • slices is the number of slide to show
