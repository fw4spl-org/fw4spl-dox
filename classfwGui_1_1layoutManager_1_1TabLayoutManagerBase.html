void fwGui::layoutManager::TabLayoutManagerBase::initialize ( ConfigurationType  configuration)
overridevirtual

Initialize tab layout manager before the creation of layout.

Example of configuration with Tab layout.

1 <service uid="subView3" type="::gui::view::SDefaultView" autoConnect="no" >
2  <gui>
3  <layout type="::fwGui::TabLayoutManager" >
4  <view caption="TabView1" />
5  <view caption="TabView2" selected="yes" />
6  <view caption="TabView3" />
7  </layout>
8  </gui>
9  <registry>
10  <view sid="view1" start="yes" />
11  <view sid="view2" start="yes" />
12  <view sid="view3" start="yes" />
13  </registry>
14 </service>
  • <layout type="::fwGui::TabLayoutManager" > : define a tab layout.
  • <view caption="TabView1" selected="center" /> : define a new view which can have the following attributes
    • caption : name of the view (display on the screen).
    • selected {yes | no}: define if the tab is the current one.
