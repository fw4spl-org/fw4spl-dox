void uiMeasurement::editor::Distance::configuring ( ) throw (fwTools::Failed) [protected, virtual]

Configure the editor.

Example of configuration 

    <service uid="distanceEditor" type="::gui::editor::IEditor" impl="::uiMeasurement::editor::Distance" autoConnect="no">
        <placeInScene uid="genericScene" />
    </service>

genericScene is the uid of the fwRenderVTK::VtkRenderService representing the generic scene which will be printed.
